## How to Get Listed

We recognize contributors in the following ways:

1. **Code Contributions**: Submit a PR (see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md))
2. **Bug Reports**: Open detailed issues on GitHub
3. **Documentation**: Improve README, guides, or examples
4. **Testing**: Validate on new platforms or with new models
5. **Domain Expertise**: Review code, suggest improvements, validate scenarios

Once your contribution is merged or validated, we'll add you here (with permission).
